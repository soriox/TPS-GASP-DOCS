# TPS Kit Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for the **Soriox's TPS Kit**, a third-person shooter framework built on [Game Animation Sample Project](https://www.fab.com/listings/880e319a-a59e-4ed2-b268-b32dac7fa016). This kit is designed to provide developers with a robust foundation for creating action-packed TPS games in Unreal Engine.

## What is Soriox's TPS Kit?

The **TPS Kit** is a modular and highly extensible framework that simplifies the development of third-person shooter mechanics. It includes:

- **Character movement & animations**
- **Shooting mechanics & weapon systems**
- **Multiplayer support**
- **Customization & extensibility**

## Why Use Soriox's TPS Kit?

- 🚀 **Quick Start** – Get up and running in minutes with prebuilt components.
- 🎯 **Optimized Performance** – Designed for smooth gameplay and scalability.
- 🔧 **Extensible** – Easily modify or extend to fit your project’s needs.
- 🎮 **Multiplayer Ready** – Built-in networking support for online play.

## Getting Started

To begin using the TPS Kit, follow these steps:

1. **Install the Kit** – Download and unzip the project.
2. **Customize & Expand** – Modify mechanics, animations, and UI to match your vision.
